## 1. Purpose

This Information Security Policy establishes the framework for protecting ALVO Platform's information assets, ensuring confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data, and maintaining compliance with applicable regulations including GDPR and SOC2 requirements.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- All employees, contractors, and third-party users
- All information systems, networks, and data
- All physical and logical access to company resources
- All processing of personal and sensitive data

## 3. Policy Statements

### 3.1 Information Classification
All information assets shall be classified into the following categories:
- **Public:** Information freely available to the public
- **Internal:** Information for internal use only
- **Confidential:** Sensitive business information
- **Restricted:** Highly sensitive information requiring strict controls

### 3.2 Access Control
- Access to information systems shall be granted based on the principle of least privilege
- All access shall be documented and regularly reviewed
- Multi-factor authentication shall be required for sensitive systems
- Access shall be revoked immediately upon termination or role change

### 3.3 Data Protection
- Personal data shall be processed in accordance with GDPR principles
- Data encryption shall be applied at rest and in transit
- Data retention periods shall be defined and enforced
- Data subject rights shall be respected and fulfilled within required timeframes

### 3.4 Incident Management
- Security incidents shall be reported immediately
- Incident response procedures shall be followed
- Root cause analysis shall be conducted for all incidents
- Lessons learned shall be documented and shared

### 3.5 Business Continuity
- Business continuity plans shall be maintained
- Regular backups shall be performed and tested
- Disaster recovery procedures shall be documented
- Recovery time objectives shall be defined and tested

### 3.6 Vendor Management
- Third-party vendors shall be assessed for security risks
- Vendor contracts shall include security requirements
- Regular vendor security reviews shall be conducted
- Vendor access shall be monitored and controlled

## 4. Responsibilities

### 4.1 Compliance Officer
- Maintain and update security policies
- Conduct compliance assessments
- Coordinate security training
- Report to Security Architect

### 4.2 Security Architect
- Design security architecture
- Approve security controls
- Oversee security implementation
- Report to CTO

### 4.3 All Employees
- Comply with security policies
- Report security incidents
- Complete security training
- Protect company information

## 5. Compliance

### 5.1 Monitoring
- Regular compliance audits shall be conducted
- Security controls shall be tested
- Policy violations shall be documented
- Corrective actions shall be tracked

### 5.2 Enforcement
- Non-compliance may result in disciplinary action
- Serious violations may result in termination
- Legal action may be taken for criminal violations

## 6. Related Documents
- Access Control Policy

## 7. Definitions
- **Information Asset:** Any data, system, or resource of value to the organization
- **Personal Data:** Any information relating to an identified or identifiable person
- **Security Incident:** Any event that compromises the confidentiality, integrity, or availability of information
